Taniti Island

Attractions

Taniti Island is home to some of the most beautiful sights you'll ever see, from our local beaches to our breathtaking volcano.

  • Mt. Ahuhai Volcano: As you first step foot on our island, your eyes will be drawn to the beautiful Mt. Ahuhai, Taniti's crown jewel. Towering over the island, this iconic landmark offers visitors a majestic view of the entire island, along with a scenic hiking trail and a museum showcasing the volcano's fiery past.
  • Numerous Local Beaches: If you want to relax and soak in the sun, please visit one of our multiple beaches. Taniti Beach is the island's landmark attractions, bustling with tourists and locals alike. Taniti also offers activities such as snorkeling, fishing, and swimming with stingrays at multiple different beaches across the island.
  • And more: There's more to see on Taniti! We have numerous more entertaining activities, such as ziplining through our lush rainforest, taking a helicopter tour of the island, and exploring our vibrant nightlife inside of Tahiti City.

Lodging

When you visit Taniti, you'll want to stay a few nights! Here are some of the best lodging options our island has to offer:

  • Yellowleaf Bay Resort: Our luxury four-star resort off of Yellowleaf Bay Beach is a favorite among our guests. They offer state of the art service and all-inclusive packages.
  • Traditional Hotels or Bed & Breakfasts: If you want a more affordable housing option, you can join one of our many small family-owned hotels or Bed & Breakfasts. These all offer more personal service and a more private experience.
  • Air BNB: Many of our locals offer up their own places through Air BNB. These options range from small apartments available for rent, to luxury beach houses right on the coastline.

Dining

Our island offers a diverse platter of restaurants and dining options!

  • Local Cuisine: You'll definitely want to experience the delicious local foods that Taniti has to offer. We have five restaurants that serve some of the best fish and rice that you'll ever eat!
  • Pan-Asian Cuisine: Taniti is the home of two of the best pan-asian restaurants around, and you'll definitely want to check them out. The hottest spot in town is Yellowleaf Cuisine, located in Yellowleaf Bay Resort (but open to the public), but be sure to make a reservation!
  • American Cuisine: For the less adventurous types, we also have several restaurants that offer traditional American food, such as chicken and steak. These range from an exquisite steakhouse to a small diner.

Important Information!

We want to ensure the safety of everyone who visits our lovely island. With that in mind, please read below:

  • While violent crime is extremely low on Taniti, with the rise of tourism also comes the rise of pickpockets and petty theft. If you see something, please make our police department aware!
  • Taniti has one hospital and several local clinics, all supported by multilingual employees and healthcare professionals. If you have a medical emergency, you will be in good hands.
  • Taniti uses the US Dollar as it's primary currency, but many of our shops and vendors also accept Euros, Yen, and major Credit Cards. We also have several banks that include currency exchanges.
