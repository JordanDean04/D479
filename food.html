Local Cuisine

Taniti features five restaurants that offer delicious fish and chips, traditional food local to Tanitians. The islanders are full of joy to share their culture and hope you enjoy your meal!

Pan-Asian Cuisine

When you visit the Pacific, you've got to try some Pan-Asian food, and no one does it better than Taniti! We have two restaurants that serve Pan-Asian food: Zen Tide and Seashell Soba.

American Cuisine

For those picky eaters, Taniti offers an array of classic American style food. Chief among these is the Mt. Ahuhai Steakhouse, one of the best steakhouses you'll ever see. There's also the competitor, Yellowleaf Resort Steakhouse, with the best steak in town. There's also a diner in Taniti City, which is much more affordable and also serves breakfast.
