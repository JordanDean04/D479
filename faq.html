Frequently Asked Questions

What is the legal drinking age?

The legal drinking age in Taniti is 18, but is not strictly enforced.
Alcohol sale is prohibited between 12:00AM and 9:00AM.

What languages are spoken in Taniti?

It depends on what area of Taniti you are in. Most younger Tanitians speak fluent English, but in rural parts of the island, you'll find few English speakers.

What power outlet types are supported in Taniti?

Power outlets are the 120 volts, the same as in the United States.
